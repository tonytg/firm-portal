# IMPACT Portal: Client Guide

**Risk, Governance & Crisis Advisory : Controlled Delivery Portal**

Welcome. This portal is the single, secure place where your engagement with IMPACT
is delivered, from the initial questionnaire through to the final reports. This
guide explains how it works, what you can do at each step, and what to look at
during this review (UAT).

> **This is a preview build for your review (UAT).** It runs on sample data so you
> can click through the full experience before your real engagement is loaded. See
> [What's simulated in this preview](#whats-simulated-in-this-preview) at the end.

---

## The idea in one minute

Your advisory work moves through a series of **stages**. The portal shows you exactly
where things stand, gives you the tasks that are yours to complete, and releases your
final deliverables in a deliberate, controlled sequence.

A few principles shape everything you'll see:

- **The advisor drives the process.** Stages do not advance on their own. IMPACT moves
  the engagement forward as each step is genuinely complete, so what you see always
  reflects real status.
- **You only see what's relevant to you.** Some stages are hands-on for you; others are
  internal analysis where you simply see that work is underway.
- **Deliverables are released, not dumped.** Your Executive Brief comes first, the full
  detail is explained in a walkthrough session, and final reports unlock only afterwards,
  always as **PDF**, never editable files.
- **Commercial milestones are respected.** An engagement opens only after activation
  (initial payment), and the final deliverables release only after the final payment.

---

## The commercial workflow

The portal follows the approved commercial sequence. Access and releases are tied to
these milestones:

**Initial Payment → Activation → Executive Brief → Walkthrough / Delivery Session → Final Payment Due → Final Deliverables Release**

- Nothing in the engagement is accessible until **Activation** (Letter of Engagement
  signed and initial payment received).
- The **Executive Brief** is released first, through the portal.
- The full detail is presented in a mandatory **Walkthrough / Delivery Session**.
- After that session, **Final Payment** falls due. The final deliverables stay locked
  until it is received, at which point they release as PDF.

## Getting around

- **Dashboard:** your home screen. It lists your engagements, each as a card showing the
  pillar, the current stage, and a progress bar.
- **Open an engagement** to see its full stage timeline.
- **Open a stage** to see its content: a questionnaire, a session, or your deliverables.
- **Header:** the IMPACT Portal bar sits at the top of every screen.
- **Footer:** every screen is marked *Confidential, IMPACT Advisory*.

### The "Client view / Advisor view" switch (preview only)

At the top right you'll see a toggle between **Client view** and **Advisor view**. This is
a **demonstration aid for this review only**. It lets you see the same engagement from
both sides:

- **Client view:** exactly what you, the client, will see.
- **Advisor view:** the internal control panel IMPACT uses (releasing deliverables,
  internal risk analysis, full stage visibility).

In the live product this toggle disappears; you simply log in and see your own view
automatically.

> **Tip for reviewers:** in Advisor view, the stage "Open" buttons now keep you in the
> advisor perspective, so you can walk the full advisor-side workflow without dropping
> back to the client view.

## Stage status at a glance

Every stage carries a clear status so you always know where it stands:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ⚪ **Not started** | This stage has not begun yet. |
| 🟡 **In progress** | Active work is happening. |
| 👁️ **Under analysis** | IMPACT is working on this internally. You see the progress, not the working detail. |
| 🔵 **Scheduled** | A session or milestone is booked. |
| 🟢 **Completed** | This stage is finished. |
| 🔒 **Locked** | Not yet available. It unlocks when the preceding gate is satisfied. |

## The two engagement types ("Pillars")

IMPACT delivers through two structured programs. Your engagement follows one of them
(and you may have both).

### Pillar 1: Risk Assessment

The progress bar advances through six approved milestones: **0% / 20% / 40% / 60% / 80% / 100%**.

| Stage | Progress | What happens | Who acts |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Activation** | 0% | Initial payment, LoE signature, and portal access. | Gate |
| **Intake** | 20% | You complete the information questionnaire (core plus your sector). | **You** |
| **Diagnosis (Internal Analysis)** | 40% | Internal red-flag analysis of your intake responses. | IMPACT (internal) |
| **Risk Identification Workshop** | (within Diagnosis) | A client-facing working session to identify and challenge risks. | **You** + IMPACT |
| **Build** | 60% | Risk register and model construction. | IMPACT (internal) |
| **Calibration Session** | 80% | Executive calibration and validation, in person. | **You** + IMPACT |
| **Output** | 100% | Your Executive Briefing Pack, the first controlled release. | **You** (download) |
| **Walkthrough / Delivery** | Delivery | Final deliverables walkthrough, then Final Payment Due before release. | **You** + IMPACT |
| **Board Delivery** | Optional | Optional board presentation and support. | **You** + IMPACT |

The **Diagnosis** milestone has two parts: the internal red-flag analysis (which you see
as *Under analysis*), and the client-facing **Risk Identification Workshop**, which you
attend and can open in the portal.

### Pillar 2: Governance Framework

A seven-stage program focused on governance architecture:

| Stage | What happens | Who acts |
|---|---|---|
| **Engagement Setup** | IMPACT opens the engagement internally. | IMPACT (internal) |
| **Governance Questionnaire & Evidence** | You complete the diagnostic and upload supporting evidence. | **You** |
| **Governance Alignment Workshop** | In-person governance workshop. | **You** + IMPACT |
| **Governance Diagnostic Exercise** | Internal governance analysis. | IMPACT (internal) |
| **Architecture Build** | IMPACT drafts the governance architecture. | IMPACT (internal) |
| **Governance Validation** | In-person validation meeting, with a read-only pre-read in the portal. | **You** + IMPACT |
| **Controlled Release** | Executive Brief, then walkthrough, then Final Payment Due, then final PDF unlock. | **You** (download) |

> **Internal stages** (Setup, Diagnostic Exercise, Architecture Build) do not appear as
> clickable items for you. They are IMPACT's internal work. You will see the engagement
> moving forward without being asked to do anything.

## What you'll do at each hands-on stage

### Activation
Shows whether your engagement is active, that is, whether the Letter of Engagement is
signed and the initial payment is received. **Until activation, the rest of the
engagement stays locked.** (IMPACT can manually activate in special cases.)

### Intake / Governance Questionnaire
Your core information-gathering step, organised into clear sections you can work through:

- **Pillar 1 Intake** covers eight core areas: business model, governance, decision
  authority, risk controls, financial exposure, workforce, reputation, and technology,
  plus a **sector-specific set** that loads automatically based on your sector (for
  example F&B, Hospital, or Construction).
- **Pillar 2 Governance Questionnaire** covers twelve governance sections (risk appetite,
  tolerance, KRIs, escalation, board oversight, and more), and asks you to **upload
  supporting evidence** alongside your answers.

You work section by section and submit when complete.

### Session stages (Risk Identification Workshop, Calibration, Validation, Walkthrough, Board)
Where a stage centres on a session, you'll see a **session panel** with the details:

- Session type (in-person or remote), date, location, and duration
- The agenda and participants
- A button to **confirm your attendance**

> **Important:** confirming attendance does **not** mark the stage complete. The stage
> completes only after the session actually happens and IMPACT uploads the sign-off. For
> the Governance Validation meeting, a **read-only pre-read** is provided in advance.

### Output / Controlled Release (your deliverables)
This is where you collect your finished reports, under a strict release discipline:

- The **Executive Brief is released first**, through the portal.
- The full architecture is explained in a **mandatory walkthrough / delivery session**.
- **Final payment then falls due**, and the **final deliverables unlock only once it is
  received**.
- Everything is **PDF only**. No editable formats are ever shared.

A deliverable that is not yet unlocked shows as 🔒 **Locked**. Once released and its gate
is satisfied, a **Download** button appears. (Locked files are protected on the server, so
they cannot be reached even with a direct link.)

## What the Advisor view shows (for context)

If you switch to **Advisor view** during this review, you'll see IMPACT's side of the same
engagement, useful for understanding the full picture:

- **Full stage visibility**, including the internal stages hidden from the client view.
- An **Advisor Control Panel** for each engagement, where IMPACT sets stage status,
  confirms the **final payment**, and releases deliverables (the Release button stays
  disabled until the required gate is met).
- **Risk Capture** (Pillar 1) : internal Cause, Event, Impact working notes that are
  **never visible to the client**.

This transparency is intentional for your review; in the live product each person only
ever sees their own role.

## What to look at during this review (UAT)

A suggested walk-through:

1. **Start on the Dashboard** and note the two sample engagements, one Pillar 1 and one
   Pillar 2, each showing a different state of progress.
2. **Open the Pillar 1 engagement** and click through the stages. Notice how completed,
   in-progress, and locked stages look and behave, and that progress sits on an approved
   milestone value.
3. **Open the Intake stage** and work through the questionnaire sections, including the
   sector-specific set. To verify completeness, open **Questionnaire Review** (linked on the
   dashboard) for a read-only list of every approved section and question.
4. **Open the Risk Identification Workshop** and review the session details and attendance
   confirmation.
5. **Open Output** to see the controlled-release deliverables list and the locked or
   unlocked states, including the Final Payment hold.
6. **Try the second (Pillar 2) engagement** to see how an engagement that is not yet fully
   activated stays locked.
7. **Flip to Advisor view**, then open stages from the dashboard to walk the advisor-side
   workflow, then switch back to Client view.

As you go, please note anything that is unclear, mislabeled, or does not behave as you'd
expect. The page or stage you were on and what you expected to happen is the most helpful
feedback.

  go-live.
